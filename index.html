What Happened?

Ayaz and his wife Aqsa were riding home on their motorcycle after work when they were hit by a car travelling well above the legal speed limit on Karachi’s Karsaz Road. Both died on the spot, leaving family, friends and an entire city in mourning. Eyewitnesses reported the driver was playing loud music during iftar and fled immediately after the crash. The vehicle, a white Honda City, was later traced back to Mehboob Asghar, who now faces charges of culpable homicide. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Frequently Asked Questions

Have the police confirmed who was driving?

According to the FIR and eyewitness accounts, the car was registered to Mehboob Asghar and he has been identified as the primary suspect. Investigation is ongoing.

Why is this case different from other Karachi road deaths?

The combination of clear CCTV evidence, under‑aged reckless driving, and the immediate social‑media outcry has turned the Karsaz Accident into a test case for traffic‑law reform in Sindh.

How can I donate to the victims’ family?

Details for verified donation channels will be posted here once legal formalities are complete. For now, sharing credible information is the best support.
