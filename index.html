Karsaz Accident — Honouring Ayaz & Aqsa

On 26 March 2025, a reckless driver ended two lives on Karachi’s Karsaz Road.
This page preserves their memory and mobilises action for safer streets.

Take Action

The Tragedy

Husband and wife Ayaz (35) and Aqsa (29) were breaking their fast roadside when a speeding Honda City ploughed into their motorcycle, killing them instantly. Witnesses said the driver was blasting music and racing another vehicle. Media reports later named the owner‑driver as Mehboob Asghar, now charged with culpable homicide. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

FAQ

Has the suspect been formally charged?

Yes. According to police statements and press coverage, Mehboob Asghar faces culpable‑homicide charges; the case is in Karachi Traffic Court. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Was the driver under‑aged?

Multiple eyewitnesses allege the driver appeared to be under 18, though official documents list him as 19. Investigation is ongoing. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
